Cat Classifier

By Jingwei Luo

When you bump into a cute cat on Instagram or in a street, you probably wonder what breed it is. However, many cat breeds are hard to be identified by human eyes, even with the help of Google. After browsing and comparing pictures of dozens of cat species, you are pretty sure what you see is a Bengal cat. However, reality can be tricky — what you actually see is a mixed breed.

The Cat Classifier is designed to help you accurately identify a cat, which uses machine learning techniques and has trained with thousands of cats’ pictures. Just simply drag a picture of a cat into the classifier below and the results will be displayed in seconds. Give it a try!
